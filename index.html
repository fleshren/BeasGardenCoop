Welcome to Bea's

Bea's Coop is here to help. There are many ways to find answers to your gardening questions, as well as find the perfect plants to fill up your garden. To view specific instructions for a plant, you must navigate to its product information page. If you have any remaining questions, feel free to contact our shop or stop by for a visit! You can even bring the plant to us for help diagnosing the problem or identifying the plant type.
