Money Tree

$45.99

Product Description

The Money Tree is a perfect indoor foliage plant to give you a tropical feel. With its braided stem and bright green palm looking leaves, it looks both like a tree and palm. Native from Mexico to northern South America, the Money Tree is also very popular in Taiwan and other East Asian countries.

Watering

About once a month, water this plant when the top inch of the soil becomes very dry. When watering, take the plant outside and water until water comes out the bottom (this is how you know all the soil is thoroughly wet! Let the plant settle outside for about an hour while any remaining excess water drips out.

Light

This plant requires low light. This means it will thrive in a darker room that only gets light for part of the day.

Difficulty

Carefree. It requires little attention in terms of water and light, and the plant itself is very resilient.

Pet Friendly

Yes
